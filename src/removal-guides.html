HuiVJope (.HuiVJope) ransomware virus - removal and decryption options

In the process of an assessment of malware samples submitted to the VirusTotal website, it has surfaced that HuiVJope is ransomware belonging to the Phobos family. HuiVJope encrypts files, modifies filenames by appending the victim's ID, an email address, and the ".HuiVJope" extension, and provides two ransom notes ("info.hta" and "info.txt").
